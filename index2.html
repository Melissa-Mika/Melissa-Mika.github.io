About Me

I am a military brat and moved every two years during my father's career in the Army. Although it was often a challenge moving around so much as a child, I now attribute my ability to adapt well in so many situations to my everchanging childhood environments. I recognize the benefits in both my personal and professional lives. I work well as a teammate but also thrive on my own. I take pride in learning and I never state nor will I believe that I know it all because I learn something new everyday. I love to code. I love to get past hurdles when they arise. I constantly want to be a better programmer. Each project I take on is both exciting and challenging and that is why I chose this career. I don't regret my previous choices regarding my occupation because those choices have been critical as to the person I am today.

Aspirations
